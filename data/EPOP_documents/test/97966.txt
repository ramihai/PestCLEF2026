Xylella fastidiosa, a bacteria that kills olive trees, present in four municipalities of Gard
An important information and awareness campaign has just been launched so that everyone can take action in Gard, France, due to the detection of the Xylella fastidiosa bacterium in 4 of its municipalities. Since November 2021, the presence of X. fastidiosa has been confirmed in Gard, near Tavel and four other villages around the A9 (2,064 hectares).
