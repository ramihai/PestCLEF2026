Xylella advances north: new outbreak also in Polignano
Negative news arrives from the Emergency Xylella institutional website: in particular of the 38 infected plants, 16 are in the countryside of Polignano, 6 in the countryside of Monopoli and 16 in the countryside of Fasano
In Puglia and Basilicata The killer bacterium July 29, 2022 Online editing BARI - Xylella advances and continues to kill olive trees. Another 38 infected plants were found between the north of Brindisi and the south of the province of Bari. An outbreak was also detected in an undamaged area around Polignano a Mare. In addition, two rosemary and a myrtle were also found to be positive. Negative news arrives from the Emergency Xylella institutional website: in particular of the 38 infected plants, 16 are in the countryside of Polignano, 6 in the countryside of Monopoli and 16 in the countryside of Fasano. Of the 16 plants in Polignano, 9 are part of a new outbreak in an undamaged area, on provincial 121 for Conversano, in an area about two km from the town, highly frequented by vehicles and heavy vehicles.
