The citrus growing debacle had been coming for 20 years, when the arrival of HLB (Huanglongbing) began to be noticed in the north of Veracruz, the noxious pest that threatens to cause total losses in orchards in the region, warned the agronomist and producer, Sergio Ruiz Valencia. “Twenty years after the phytosanitary fight began in this region, the proper approach had not been given in this region. There is a culture in the field that means that many programs do not pass, but today HLB is a big problem that is going to detonate and, by its nature, it is going to control the planting of citrus fruits," he said. He added that, in the United States, the first leading producer of citrus fruits and with state-of-the-art technology, it has not been able to counteract the disease, which “already caused enormous losses last year of hundreds of thousands of tons and deaths in thousands of orchards. So, what awaits us?", he questioned before the null results of the phytosanitary campaigns. Likewise, he admitted that, as a delegate of Plant Health, he even feels sorry, since there is no effective strategy or coordination with the producers. “As a representative of Plant Health, another position that is sad, for me there was no communication or there is no communication, there are no resources or they are not required. The mere truth is sad, especially me, as an agricultural technician," he emphasized. Ruiz Valencia pointed out that he had a work plan to be able to, at least, “make a block and say: we are going to invest so many millions of pesos against the plague, but I don't know what happened. It is worrisome, the HLB is already in all of Tihuatlán and there are no actions. We need to apply ourselves because this is very serious”, he stressed.
