The mayor of El Retén asked Minagricultura to attend to an emergency due to pests that affect small growers
In the capital of the Republic, the Minister of Agriculture, Cecilia López, received the representative to the Chamber, Hernando Guida and the mayor of El Retén, The mayor of El Retén asked Minagricultura to attend to an emergency due to pests that affect small growers In the capital of the Republic, the Minister of Agriculture, Cecilia López, received the representative to the Chamber, Hernando Guida and the mayor of El Retén, Jorge Eliecer Serrano; who expressed their concern about the situation experienced by small palm, banana and other producers, in the face of the pests that afflict these plantations. “She was very receptive to hear about our initiatives based in that Ministry in order to help our men and women in the countryside, a project on bovine repopulation and the planting of Cocoa to face the crisis generated by the Rot of the Cogollo-PC-, which has severely hit palm growers, is in principle our great bet before his office”, said the mayor. For his part, the representative to the Chamber, Hernando Guida, referred to the bill that is intended to be promoted. “We have the proposal through a bill that the Government of President Gustavo Petro declares of 'National Social Interest' and as a health priority the prevention, mitigation, eradication, containment and renewal of plantain and banana wilt ( Musáceas), Huanglongbing disease (HLB) or Yellow Dragon, which affects citrus, and Bud Rot PC, a lethal enemy of oil palms”, explained Representative Guida.
