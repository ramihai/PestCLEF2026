Dreaded tomato virus spreads in Europe - chief inspector fears that Finnish crops may be affected
The most dangerous of all pests and plant diseases in tomato crops, the ToBRFV virus, is now spreading rapidly in Europe. It is a matter of time before the virus is detected here, says chief inspector. The financial damage could be great in the tomato kingdom of Närpes.
