Xylella: prevention with a biosensor
An electronic platform to prevent Xylella, Researchers from the University of Bari and the Cnr, in collaboration with Agritest S.r.l., have created an electronic biosensor to find infected plants, quickly and accurately revealing the presence of a single bacterium of Xylella fastidiosa directly in the field.
